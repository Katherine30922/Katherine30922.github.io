Expense Tracker is a simple and handy tool which helps you track every expense and improve the financial health of your budget, staying on top of your cash flow .

step 1

First, enter your expense detail in the input box at the top.
*All blanks are required.
*Amount cannot be negative.

step 2

Then, based on the type of expense, select a category that most closely matches.

step 3

Finally, an expense breakdown will be automatically added to the list below.
On the right is a table that keeps track of the sums for each category and records the total cost.
